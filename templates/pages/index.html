The Easiest Digital Menu System for Modern Restaurants

About NoMenu

NoMenu is an easily accessible platform that helps your eatery to create and manage a real-time, digital menu for your customers. In addition to helping you ditch the rising costs of traditional paper items, NoMenu provides a modern take on the original menu.

NoMenu is a digital menu management system designed for modern restaurants to use amidst the COVID-19 pandemic. NoMenu serves as the better alternative to other online menu platforms, including Grubhub and Seamless. While online menu management systems are seemingly popping up everywhere, NoMenu is able to stand out from the crowd. Numerous convenient features and a simplistic, easy-to-use software interface gives NoMenu a distinct edge over the competition. In addition to providing the dynamic nature of a digital menu to consumers, NoMenu goes a step further and makes it easier for your eatery to adapt to the new normal.

Benefits of a digital menu

Healthy

NoMenu requires just that - no menu. Providing a contactless experience for both customers and employees is our priority.

Eco-Friendly

In contrast to a traditional paper menu, digital menus reduce physical waste while taking less time to design, implement, or update.

Dynamic

Control exactly what your customers can see using digital controls to quickly add or remove menu items. Updating a digital menu like NoMenu takes just seconds.

Traditonal Menu vs. QR Menu

Why should your restaurant choose NoMenu over a traditional paper menu?

Better experience

It provides a better user experience than a regular paper menu. By making use of simplistic yet colorful design elements, NoMenu presents your menu items to customers in an eye-catching, easy-to-read format.

Web Based

NoMenu is entirely web-based. There’s no need to download a certain app - the accessibility of NoMenu is unmatched. Simply use any mobile or computer internet browser to make menu item changes, configure scheduling times, and more.

Analytics

NoMenu comes equipped with Google Analytics support giving you the ability to measure your most popular menu items and peak operating times.

How NoMenu Works

Do your customers spend unbearable amounts of time poring over your paper menus?

Are your customers concerned about the possibility of becoming sick with COVID-19 after using a physical menu?
